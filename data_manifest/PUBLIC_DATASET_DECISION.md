## Required before benchmark execution

1. Confirm dataset license and access terms.
2. Record citation and download/access date.
3. Document signal format, sampling rate, and annotation availability.
4. Define train/test or evaluation split if needed.
5. Record any preprocessing assumptions.

## Boundary

Dataset selection must happen before expanding clinical or performance claims.
