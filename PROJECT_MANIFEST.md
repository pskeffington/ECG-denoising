# Project Manifest

## Repository role

This repository is a biomedical AI evaluation workspace for ECG denoising, signal-quality benchmarking, and clinically relevant morphology-preservation methods.

## Current status

Early-stage methods development.

## Public CV wording

ECG denoising and signal-quality benchmark scaffold aligned with open medical data and reproducible biomedical AI evaluation; dataset, noise classes, methods, and results tables remain pending.

## Primary objects

- Public ECG dataset manifest
- Noise taxonomy
- Baseline denoising methods
- Signal-quality metrics
- Morphology-preservation metrics
- Result-table templates
- Minimal reproducible benchmark script

## Roadmap priority

Priority 5. Biomedical AI evaluation proof packet after higher-priority manuscript and infrastructure-risk work are stabilized.

## Validation boundary

Do not claim clinical performance or diagnostic improvement until reproducible benchmark results exist and morphology-preservation checks are documented.
